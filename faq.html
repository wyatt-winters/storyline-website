FAQ

How do I get started?
  1. Give me your photos, either by uploading digital images to a shared drive or giving me prints to scan. I'll edit the pictures, design the book and craft the words to tell your story. All prints will be promptly returned.
  2. If you want captions and narrative copy, we will work on those together.
  3. Preview the project, make edits, approve and order.
What is the pricing?
  • $4.50 per page with 20-page minimum plus tax (Storyline services), plus charges for photo book vendors (see below).
  • Payment for printing, tax and shipping for photo book vendor. I use a variety of photo book vendors, including Shutterfly, Mixbook, and Vistaprint. I will research and recommend the best one for your project. Pricing varies according to the client's choice of book size, number of pages and other specs. Any discounts offered by the photo book vendors will be applied.
  • Payment due upon placement of order, based on estimated number of pages and other specs. Estimate will be supplied before work on the book begins. Adjustments can be made if the client wants to add more pages.
  • ACH, Venmo, cash and Zelle preferred. We also accept Visa and Mastercard.
When can I expect my delivery?

We will establish deadlines and delivery dates once you have made a payment.

Who prints the book?

I use a variety of photo book sites, including Shutterfly, Mixbook, and Vistaprint. I will research and recommend the best one for your project.

What about other products, such as greeting cards, invitations and home decor?

Most sites offer a variety of products, and I can help with those, also. Pricing fluctuates, so I charge $10 for each image + the price for the product, tax and delivery.

Just tell me your idea, and I'll get to work!
