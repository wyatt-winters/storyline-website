Photo sharing made simple

It can be puzzling

The easiest workflow is to collect your images in one Google Drive folder and share that folder with Storyline.

How to prepare your photos

  1. Search your camera roll, desktop folders, text threads, and cloud albums for the pictures that belong in your project.
  2. Create a folder named for your book, like Smith Anniversary Book.
  3. Drop your favorites into that folder. Rule of thumb: 150–350 photos per book (maximum 500). It's fine if there are more photos than are used. Please name them to reflect information, rather than using the default JPEG name — for example, if you're making a book about a trip to Japan, name your photos by location: Tokyo, Osaka, and so on.
  4. Upload the folder to Google Drive.
  5. Share access with Helen so Storyline can begin editing and designing.

If you are unsure how to organize things, send what you have and give us a call. Storyline can help shape the photo selection from there.

Pricing basics

Clear and honest pricing

  • Storyline services are $4.50 per page with a 20-page minimum, plus tax.
  • Printing, tax, and delivery are billed separately by the photo book vendor. Storyline researches and recommends the best fit, including Mixbook, Shutterfly, and Vistaprint, and any vendor discounts are applied.
  • Payment is due when the order is placed, based on the estimated number of pages and book specs. An estimate is supplied before work begins, and adjustments can be made if more pages are added.
  • ACH, Visa, Mastercard, Venmo, PayPal, and cash are accepted. ACH is preferred to avoid credit card charges.
